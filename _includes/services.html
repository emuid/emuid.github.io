Services

Through the iterative design process, EMU:ID will ensure that options are tested and agreed whilst progressing through a project lifecycle.
Whether engaged with our clients in part, or the whole lifecycle, our expertise in achieving clear milestones will be beneficial to any building project.

Listen

Interview stakeholders and establish a clear vision.

Capture

Observe current undertakings with space utilisation audits and legacy reviews.

Analyse

Benchmark findings and build the brief.

Challenge

Challenge current practices, and identify how they are relevant to your organisation.

Workshop

Explore the options, analyse the building and innovate the end users.

Concept

Launch the big idea. Presenting the Initial Design Proposal.

Options

Capture the feedback, look at the details and refine the options.

Develop

Build on the idea, look at the specifics and create a solution.

Present

Present the solution; agree relevant iteration of sign off before continuing further development of the scheme.

Costs

Liaise with suppliers, balance the budget, analyse tender submissions, negotiate best price.

Project Management

Coordinate with specialist contractors, undertake sample reviews, organise mock-ups or pilot spaces. Programme the delivery.

Deliver

Carry out site management, highlight snagging issues and coordinate O&M manuals. Undertake an end project review, with a lessons learned and future aspirations workshop.
