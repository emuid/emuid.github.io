Services

Through the iterative design process, EMU Interior Design will ensure that options are tested and agreed whilst progressing through a project lifecycle.

Whether engaged with our clients in part, or the whole life cycle, our expertise in achieving clear milestones will be beneficial to any building project.

Prepare

Listen / Capture / Analyse

  • Interview Stakeholders and establish a clear vision
  • Observe current undertakings with space utilisation audits
  • Benchmark findings and build the brief

Inspire

Challenge / Workshop / Concept

  • Challenge current practices and identify how they are relevant to your organisation
  • Explore the options, analyse the building and innovate the end users
  • Launch the big idea. Presenting the initial design proposal

Deliver

Project Management / Deliver

  • Coordinate with specialist contractors
  • Undertake sample reviews, organize mock ups or pilot spaces
  • Programme the delivery
  • On site presence
  • Highlight snagging issues
  • Co-ordinate O&M manuals
  • Project review complete with lessons learned and future aspirations workshop

Design

Options / Develop / Present / Costs

  • Capture the feeback, look at the details and refine the options
  • Build on the idea, look at the specifics and create a solution
  • Present the solution; agree relevant iteration of sign off
  • Liaise with suppliers, balance the budget, analyse tender submissions, negotiate best price
